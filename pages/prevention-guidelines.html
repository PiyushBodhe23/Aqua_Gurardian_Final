WHO Water Safety Guidelines

Official recommendations from the World Health Organization

Water Treatment Methods

Boiling

Bring water to a rolling boil for at least 1 minute to kill pathogens.

  • Use clean container
  • Bring to vigorous boil
  • Maintain boil for 1+ minutes
  • Cool naturally without recontamination

Chlorination

Use chlorine-based disinfectants to treat water effectively.

  • Use appropriate chlorine concentration
  • Mix thoroughly
  • Wait 30 minutes before use
  • Store in clean, covered container

Filtration

Use certified water filters to remove contaminants.

  • Choose appropriate filter type
  • Follow manufacturer instructions
  • Regular maintenance required
  • Replace filters as recommended

Hygiene Practices

Hand Hygiene

Proper handwashing to prevent disease transmission.

  • Wash with soap and clean water
  • Scrub for at least 20 seconds
  • Wash before eating and after toilet use
  • Use alcohol-based sanitizer when water unavailable

Food Safety

Prevent water-borne diseases through food safety.

  • Wash fruits and vegetables with safe water
  • Cook food thoroughly
  • Avoid cross-contamination
  • Store food at safe temperatures

Personal Hygiene

Maintain personal cleanliness to prevent infections.

  • Bathe regularly with safe water
  • Keep living areas clean
  • Proper waste disposal
  • Avoid swimming in contaminated water

Home Water Safety

Water Storage

Safe practices for storing drinking water at home.

  • Use clean, covered containers
  • Store away from contaminants
  • Use separate containers for different purposes
  • Regular cleaning of storage containers

Point-of-Use Treatment

Treating water immediately before consumption.

  • Install household water filters
  • Use water purification tablets
  • UV disinfection systems
  • Regular testing of treated water

Sanitation Facilities

Proper sanitation to prevent water contamination.

  • Proper toilet facilities
  • Safe distance from water sources
  • Regular maintenance
  • Proper waste management
