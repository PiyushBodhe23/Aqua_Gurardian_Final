About Aqua Guardian Health

Pioneering water quality monitoring solutions for a healthier world

Our Mission

To provide accessible, real-time water quality monitoring solutions that empower communities to prevent water-borne diseases and ensure safe water for all.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to clean, safe water, and communities are equipped with the tools to monitor and maintain water quality standards.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've helped monitor water quality for over 500 communities and prevented thousands of water-related illnesses through early detection.

Our Story

Aqua Guardian Health was founded in 2018 by a team of environmental scientists, public health experts, and technology innovators who recognized the critical need for accessible water quality monitoring solutions.

After witnessing the devastating impact of water-borne diseases in underserved communities, our founders set out to develop a comprehensive platform that would democratize water quality monitoring and empower communities to take proactive measures.

Today, we partner with government agencies, NGOs, and communities worldwide to implement our monitoring systems and provide actionable insights for water safety management.
