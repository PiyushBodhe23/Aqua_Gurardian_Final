Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How accurate are your water quality monitoring systems?

Our monitoring systems maintain 95% accuracy for most water quality parameters. We use certified sensors and regular calibration to ensure reliable data. All systems undergo rigorous testing before deployment.

Can individuals purchase your monitoring equipment?

Yes, we offer individual and household monitoring kits. These are designed for home use and provide real-time water quality data through our mobile app. Contact our sales team for pricing and availability.

How quickly can you deploy monitoring systems?

Standard deployment takes 2-4 weeks depending on location and system requirements. For emergency situations, we offer rapid deployment services that can be activated within 48-72 hours.

Do you provide training for using your systems?

Yes, comprehensive training is included with all our system deployments. We provide both in-person and virtual training sessions, along with detailed documentation and ongoing support.

What regions do you currently serve?

We currently operate in North America, Europe, and parts of Asia and Africa. We're continuously expanding our reach. Contact us to check availability in your specific region.
